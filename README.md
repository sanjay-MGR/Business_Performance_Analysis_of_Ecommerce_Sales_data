## 📋 Project Overview

This project performs a comprehensive **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on an Ecommerce Superstore sales dataset to uncover key business insights and provide actionable recommendations for improving profitability.

Despite healthy sales volume, the company faces **low profit margins** due to excessive discounting and underperforming product categories.

---

## 🎯 Key Findings

- **Total Sales**: ~$2.30 Million
- **Total Profit**: $286,397
- **Overall Profit Margin**: **12.45%**
- **Average Discount**: **15.62%**
- **Loss-Making Orders**: 1,871 (**18.7%** of total orders)

### Critical Insights:
- **Technology** category is the most profitable (especially **Copiers, Phones & Accessories**).
- **Furniture** category (especially **Tables**) is the biggest loss driver.
- Discounts **above 40%** strongly correlate with **negative profits**.
- **Standard Class** shipping is the most cost-effective.
- **Consumer** segment drives highest sales but has lower margins.

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (0–30 days)
- Implement strict discount caps (maximum 40%)
- Review and restrict heavy discounts on **Furniture – Tables**
- Audit all 1,871 loss-making orders

### Short-Term (1–3 months)
- Focus product portfolio on high-profit **Technology** items
- Introduce category-specific dynamic pricing rules
- Shift sales incentives from revenue to **profit-based**

### Long-Term (3–12 months)
- Expand successful strategies from high-profit regions
- Develop premium Technology product lines
- Implement Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) tracking

**Projected Outcome**: Increase profit margin from **12.45% to 22–26%**
